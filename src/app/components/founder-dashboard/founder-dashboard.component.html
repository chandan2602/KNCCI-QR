Welcome, Founder!

This is your personalized space to manage and track your startup journey. Here, you can submit your startup idea, choose up to three relevant incubators, and monitor the status of your submission - whether it's under review, approved, or rejected. Once submitted, your idea will be securely shared with the selected incubators for evaluation. You’ll also be able to view detailed feedback, including ratings and comments provided by the incubators. Please note, only one idea can be submitted at a time until it’s reviewed. Use this dashboard to stay updated, refine your vision, and connect with the right ecosystem to bring your innovation to life.
