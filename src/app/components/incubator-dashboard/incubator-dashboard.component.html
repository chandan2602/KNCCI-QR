Welcome, Incubator!

This dashboard is your central hub to manage and evaluate startup ideas submitted by aspiring founders. You’ll receive ideas relevant to your sector, complete with detailed information, supporting documents, videos, and photos. For each submission, you can review the content, assign a rating (0–10), provide feedback in the comments section, and take action by approving or rejecting the idea. Approved ideas will automatically be routed to suitable investors for further consideration. Track the number of received, approved, and rejected ideas at a glance. Use this space to mentor, support, and accelerate promising startups that align with your mission.
